cyber ROOT 21

Posted on October 17, 2021

cyber ROOT 21 is an online webinar that tries to tackle cyber security from different aspects, taking both the technological and the business aspect. The event will be held on Friday 29th October 2021 between 09:30 and 16:00. Registration is free, click here for a full programme.

Together with colleagues from the University of Malta, Robert Abela and Chris Farrugia will be presenting a session titled "The Cyber Research Domain: From Threat, to Tool, to Training".
