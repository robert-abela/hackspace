Demystifying the Dark Web

In recent times, the dark web and the activities associated with it have been given exposure in both national and international news reports. The main objective of the dark web is without any doubt anonymity. Like most things in life, anonymity can be used and abused.

During this 1 hour session, which will be held on Wednesday 15th December at 13.30, we shall be describing the architecture of the dark web, its use as an intelligence source and the challenges it presents to law enforcement investigating online crime.
