Curiousity got you here, we hope you stay!

Hacking is about looking at problems in a different way, maybe a way that no one has thought of. Curiosity is often the spark that gets you in, the challenge is what makes you keep trying and the thrill of succeeding makes you want to do it all over again.

MCAST HackSpace is a space for tinkerers and security-curious students. The aim is to go beyond the media hype and the scary titles, to understand the technologies, the mistakes that make us vulnerable and learn best practices to improve our security hygiene.

You are invited to join, you will love it!
