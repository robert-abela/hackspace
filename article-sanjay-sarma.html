Session with Prof. Sanjay Sarma

Posted on November 13

Yesterday we had the opportunity to attend a session with Professor Sanjay Sarma from Massachusetts Institute of Technology. During the session he spoke about his journey and past work, with particular attention to the security of Sensors, IoT and RFID. Towards the end he spent time talking to MCAST students and answering their questions, especially about the research topics they are considering.

For more information about his work, see: https://openlearning.mit.edu/about/our-team/sanjay-sarma.
