Personal Security Audits

We will be doing trial sessions called "Personal Security Audit", meant for a small number of members. This first round will serve as a template for future sessions, even out of IICT. The two sessions will take please next week:

  • Mon 8th at 10.30-11.30
  • Wed 10th at 10.00-11.00

The following will be covered:

  • Laptop: A/V on, updates, non-admin permissions
  • Smartphone: OS version, apps updated, unused apps and permissions
  • Social media: profile "from eyes of future employer"
  • Google: search your own name using Ninja skills
  • General: unique pws, 2fa, have i been pwnd?
